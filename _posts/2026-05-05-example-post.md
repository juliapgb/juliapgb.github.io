## What about formatting?

Bullet points can be made like this:

 - Bullet 1
 - Bullet 2

Numbered lists:

 1. Item 1
 2. Item 2

Tables like this:

| Column 1 | Column 2 |
|----------|----------|
| Item 1   |          |
| Item 2   | Item 3   |

etc.
